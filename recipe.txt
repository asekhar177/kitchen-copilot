Recipe: Quick Paneer Butter Masala
Ingredients: Paneer cubes, onion paste, tomato puree, ginger-garlic paste, heavy cream, butter, garam masala.

Steps:
1. Melt butter in the pan and sauté the ginger-garlic paste and onion paste until golden brown.
2. Add the tomato puree and spices, simmering until the oil starts to separate from the paste.
3. Pour in the heavy cream to create a smooth, rich gravy.
4. Add the paneer cubes and simmer gently for 5 minutes until heated through.
